ENGINNERING NOTES WEBSITE

Our Mission

Engineering Notes is a remarkable online platform dedicated to providing students with comprehensive, high-quality handwritten notes at absolutely no cost. Our mission is to support and empower students in their academic journey by offering a vast repository of educational resources. As an open-source project, we warmly welcome and encourage contributions from individuals like you who are passionate about education.


Through the collaborative efforts of our contributors, we continuously expand and enhance our collection of educational materials. We understand the value of diverse perspectives, and we encourage individuals from different backgrounds and experiences to join our project. By leveraging the power of open source, we foster a dynamic and inclusive community that thrives on knowledge sharing and collaboration.

About Chaibasa Engineering College

Chaibasa Engineering College, established in 2013 by the Jharkhand Government, is a prestigious institution of higher education. Operated under the guidance of the esteemed Techno India Group, the college is recognized by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).
